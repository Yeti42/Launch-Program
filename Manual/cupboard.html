In addition to the tutor buckets and Launch games bins, we have added many games and activities to enhance the Launch experience. This section provides a guide to their location. Please replace them correctly – your fellow tutors will thank you, and everyone will save time when they do not have to hunt around for items!

Top Shelf

  • One tutor bucket.
  • Picture cards box.
  • Abacus.
  • Shape & learn letter sounds.
  • Roll & Write Word Game.
